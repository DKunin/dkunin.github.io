Create New Node

To create a new node, click and drag from an existing node, then release.

Move a Node

To move a node, mouse over it and then click and drag the move icon in the bottom right corner.

Remove a Node

To remove a node, mouse over it and click the close icon in the top right corner.

Edit a Node

To edit a node, mouse over it, click once, and type to edit.

Link Nodes

To create a connection between two nodes, click and drag from one node to another. Wait until you see the link icon, then release.

Unlink Nodes

To remove the connection between two nodes, mouse over the connection until you see the scissors, then click to unlink.

Panning the canvas

To move the canvas, click and drag from any blank space.
